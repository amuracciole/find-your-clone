Search your clone!

Dare to find your clone or find out if you are unique!

Have you ever been mistaken for someone else? With more than 7.8 billion people, do you think you are unique? or can there be someone similar to you in another part of the world? These are questions you may have asked yourself in the past, so this project tries to find your clone in the world.

Do you know the story of Paul McCartney? If not, google "Paul is dead". We don't know if this story is real and we will never know, but if so, it confirms that somewhere in the world there is someone extremely similar to you who could impersonate you. Paul McCartney found his William Campbell... you find yours.
